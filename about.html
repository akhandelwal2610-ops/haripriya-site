About Us · हमारे बारे में

Where modern medicine meets a family clinic's warmth.

For patients across Meerut, Haripriya Heart & Eye Care Center is a place where two of medicine's most technology-driven specialties — cardiology and ophthalmology — are practiced with honesty, patience, and the time each person deserves.

एक छत के नीचे हृदय एवं नेत्र रोगों की आधुनिक, सुलभ और भरोसेमंद चिकित्सा।

Our story

Haripriya Heart & Eye Care Center was founded by a doctor couple — Dr. Hari Om Tyagi, an interventional cardiologist, and Dr. Jeenu Priya Tyagi, an ophthalmic surgeon — with a simple belief: that good care should not feel rushed.

Our clinic on Garh Road brings together two specialties that usually live in separate hospitals. For a family, that means one trusted address for an ECG today and a cataract consult next week. For an older patient, it means fewer trips, clearer answers, and doctors who speak plainly — in Hindi or English, whichever is easier.

We invest where it matters: diagnostics that catch problems early, surgical techniques that minimise downtime, and consultations that are long enough to understand what is actually going on.

What we stand for

Our principles of care

Four commitments that guide everything from a 10-minute consultation to a 2-hour procedure.

Honesty first

We recommend tests and procedures only when they change management. No upselling, no scare tactics.

Evidence-based

Treatment follows current national and international guidelines — explained in language you understand.

Time that heals

Safety by design

Facilities · सुविधाएँ

Under one roof on Garh Road

Diagnostic-grade equipment, two dedicated OPD streams, and an operating infrastructure built for the two specialties we practice.

Cardiology suite

  • 12-lead ECG with computerised reporting
  • 2D & Doppler Echocardiography
  • Treadmill Test (TMT) for stress evaluation
  • 24-hour Holter monitoring
  • Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitoring (ABPM)
  • Cardiac consultation & procedure coordination

Ophthalmology suite

  • Automated refraction & visual acuity testing
  • Slit-lamp biomicroscopy & fundus evaluation
  • Non-contact & applanation tonometry (glaucoma screening)
  • A-scan / B-scan biometry for IOL calculation
  • MICS (Micro-Incision Cataract Surgery) capability
  • World-class modular Eye OT for day-care cataract and eye procedures
  • Post-operative care & refractive follow-up
