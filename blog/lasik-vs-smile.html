LASIK vs. SMILE: Choosing the Best Laser Vision Correction in NCR

By Dr. Jeenu Priya Tyagi • Published: Feb 2025

For thousands of young people across Delhi NCR and West UP, wearing heavy glasses or dealing with dry contact lenses is a massive inconvenience. Laser vision correction offers a permanent exit ticket. But which procedure is right for you: Traditional LASIK or the newer SMILE approach?

How Bladeless LASIK Works

LASIK is the most famous laser surgery in the world. A tiny, ultra-thin protective flap is created on the cornea using a precise femtosecond laser. The surgeon folds back the flap, and an excimer laser painlessly reshapes the underlying cornea to fix myopia, hyperopia, or astigmatism. The flap is then put back and heals naturally.

How SMILE is Different

SMILE (Small Incision Lenticule Extraction) is a newer, flapless technique. Instead of creating a flap, a laser creates a tiny disc of tissue inside the cornea, which is then pulled out through a microscopic 2mm keyhole incision.

Which One Should You Choose?

  • Visual Recovery: LASIK offers incredibly fast "wow" factor visual recovery, with most patients seeing 20/20 by the very next morning.
  • Dry Eyes: SMILE is often better for patients with pre-existing severe dry eye, as it is flapless and cuts fewer corneal nerves.
  • Astigmatism: LASIK is generally superior for correcting complex high astigmatism.
