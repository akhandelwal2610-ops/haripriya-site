# Haripriya Site — Client Review & Approval Checklist

Use this checklist for the **first client review** (comments + approvals), and to close decisions like the final domain.

---

## Domain & hosting (decision required)

- **Primary domain (final website URL)**: approve **`haripriyacare.in`** as the canonical domain.
- **Backup domain** (optional): decide if we also register **`haripriyacare.com`** (and redirect it to `.in`).
- **Email address for contact links**: confirm the clinic email to publish (currently uses a Gmail fallback).
- **Google Business / Maps link**: confirm the exact Google Maps listing to link (place name + pin).
- **Sharing link for review**: confirm the GitHub Pages preview URL is acceptable for review.

---

## Brand & messaging (decision required)

- **Tagline (English)**: approve the homepage tagline.
- **Tagline (Hindi)**: approve the Hindi tagline.
- **Tone**: confirm if the clinic wants “premium/modern” tone or “warm/family clinic” tone (we can lean either way consistently).
- **Key claims**: approve phrases like:
  - “Advanced equipment”
  - “World‑class modular Eye OT”
  - “No upselling / honesty first”
  - “Evidence‑based”
  - Any “years of experience” figures

---

## Content correctness (must verify)

- **Doctors’ names, degrees, and specialties**:
  - Dr. Hari Om Tyagi: degrees, specialty, memberships, OPD timings
  - Dr. Jeenu Priya Tyagi: degrees, specialty, fellowships, OPD timings
- **Address**: “67, 1st Floor, Harsh Commercial Park, Garh Road, Ajanta Colony, Meerut 250004” (confirm wording).
- **Phone + WhatsApp number**: confirm it’s the correct number for bookings.
- **OPD timings**: confirm morning/evening and weekly off-days.
- **Services list**: confirm we should list all procedures publicly (or keep some internal).
- **Facilities**: confirm equipment/facilities list (including **modular Eye OT**) is accurate.
- **Emergency disclaimer**: approve the emergency guidance language and placement.

---

## Design / UX (approval required)

- **Homepage first impression**: does it feel trustworthy and premium for a medical clinic?
- **Navigation**: prefer a **single-page** homepage with sections, or a **multi-page** site style everywhere?
- **Language toggle**: confirm if the website should be:
  - bilingual site-wide, or
  - bilingual only on the homepage
- **Photos**:
  - approve doctor photos (lighting, crop, background)
  - decide if we add clinic interior photos on homepage
- **Call-to-action buttons**: approve the primary CTA wording (“Book Appointment” vs “Call / WhatsApp”).

---

## SEO & metadata (approval + technical)

- **Canonical domain consistency**: confirm all pages should use `haripriyacare.in` in canonical + OpenGraph.
- **Page titles**: approve page titles for homepage + services pages.
- **Blog strategy**:
  - approve topics list
  - approve whether we publish full articles now or later
  - approve author naming (“Dr.” vs “Haripriya Centre”)

---

## Legal / compliance (decision required)

- **Privacy note**: approve wording that the form does not store data on a server (WhatsApp opens locally).
- **Medical disclaimers**: approve emergency/education disclaimers on blog pages.
- **Consent language**: approve consent checkbox wording on the contact form.

---

## Post-approval deliverables (after decisions)

- **Connect domain + SSL** to GitHub Pages (or migrate to another host if desired).
- **301 redirects** from any secondary domains.
- **Finalize blog** (publish articles and remove any “placeholder” pages from indexing).
- **Performance pass**: image formats (WebP/AVIF), width/height attributes, font optimization.
- **Accessibility pass**: focus states, nav ARIA, language toggle persistence.
