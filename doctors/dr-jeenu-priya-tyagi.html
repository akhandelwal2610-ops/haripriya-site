About Dr. Tyagi

Dr. Jeenu Priya Tyagi is a highly skilled ophthalmologist and refractive surgeon with specialised training in Phaco cataract surgery, corneal diseases, retina, glaucoma, and laser vision correction. She brings precision and compassion to every patient encounter, making complex eye procedures feel approachable and safe.

At Haripriya Heart & Eye Care Centre, Dr. Tyagi offers the full spectrum of eye care — from routine consultations and glasses prescriptions to advanced surgical procedures including Micro Incision Cataract Surgery (MICS), LASIK, Phakic IOL, and management of retinal conditions like diabetic retinopathy.

Areas of Expertise

🔭 Cataract Surgery (Phaco / MICS)

Stitchless Micro Incision Cataract Surgery with premium foldable IOL implants. No injections, no pain, same-day discharge.

✨ Laser Vision Correction

LASIK and SMILE for permanent correction of myopia, hyperopia, and astigmatism. Freedom from glasses and contact lenses.

👁️ Glaucoma Management

Early detection and treatment of glaucoma (काला पानी) including medication, SLT laser, and surgical drainage.

🦮 Retina Services

Diabetic retinopathy, retinal detachment, macular degeneration, and intravitreal injection therapy.

🌐 Cornea & Transplantation

Corneal ulcer management, keratoconus treatment, and corneal transplantation (PKP / DALK / DSEK).

💎 Phakic IOL Surgery

Vision correction for patients with very high spectacle power who are not suitable for LASIK.

💄 Oculoplasty

Eyelid surgeries, ptosis correction, and management of lacrimal (tear duct) conditions.

🔬 Comprehensive Eye Care

Routine consultations, refraction, fundus evaluation, OCT imaging, and laser photocoagulation.
