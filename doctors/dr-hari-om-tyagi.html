About Dr. Tyagi

Dr. Hari Om Tyagi is one of Meerut's most experienced interventional cardiologists, with over two decades dedicated to diagnosing and treating complex heart conditions. After completing his DM in Cardiology, he returned to Meerut with a mission: to bring tertiary-level cardiac care to patients who would otherwise need to travel to Delhi or Lucknow.

At Haripriya Heart & Eye Care Centre, Dr. Tyagi performs the full spectrum of interventional cardiology procedures — from diagnostic angiography to angioplasty, stenting, pacemaker implantation, ICD and CRT device therapy, and emergency cardiac intervention.

Areas of Expertise

🫀 Coronary Angiography

Diagnostic catheterization to map coronary arteries and identify blockages with precision.

💉 Coronary Angioplasty & Stenting

Balloon-assisted opening of blocked arteries with permanent stent placement.

🔋 Pacemaker Implantation

Single and dual-chamber pacemakers for bradycardia and heart block conditions.

⚡ ICD & CRT Devices

Implantable Cardioverter-Defibrillators and Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy for advanced heart conditions.

🩺 Heart Failure Management

Comprehensive medical and interventional management of acute and chronic heart failure.

📈 ECG, 2D Echo & Holter

Full non-invasive cardiac diagnostics including TMT, Holter monitoring, and ABPM.

🚨 Emergency Cardiac Care

Immediate intervention for acute myocardial infarction (heart attack) and cardiac emergencies.

💊 Hypertension & Lipid Management

Long-term management of hypertension, dyslipidemia, and cardiovascular risk reduction.

🫁 Peripheral Vascular Disease

Diagnosis and management of peripheral arterial and venous conditions.
